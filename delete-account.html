Delete Your CRA Mobile Account

We respect your privacy. If you wish to delete your CRA Mobile account and associated data, please follow the instructions below.

Steps to Request Account Deletion:

  • Open the CRA Mobile app and log in to your account.
  • Go to Settings > Account Management > Delete Account.
  • Tap "Request Account Deletion" and confirm the action.

Data That Will Be Deleted:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • App usage history

Data That May Be Retained:

  • Technical logs and system records for up to 30 days (for legal and security purposes)

Retention Period:

Most user data is permanently deleted within 7 days. Some technical logs may be retained for up to 30 days.
